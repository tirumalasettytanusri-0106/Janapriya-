Leadership

The Visionaries

Mr. Ravinder Reddy
Founder & Chairman

An engineer by training and a visionary by nature, Mr. Ravinder Reddy established Janapriya with a singular purpose: making home ownership an accessible reality for everyone without sacrificing structural integrity.

His leadership has steered the company through nearly four decades of market evolution, always anchored by his commitment to value-based engineering.

Mr. Kranti Kiran Reddy
Managing Director

Bringing a global perspective to Janapriya, Kranti Kiran Reddy holds a Civil Engineering degree from North Carolina State University, USA.

His focus on cutting-edge construction technology and optimizing speed through industrial-scale processes has revolutionized operational efficiency, ensuring timely delivery of premium upscale projects.
